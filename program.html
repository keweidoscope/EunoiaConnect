The Art Program

Process over product. Connection over perfection.

Our weekly art sessions are designed with one guiding philosophy: the goal is never a finished painting. The goal is a child who tried and learned something new, felt safe doing it, and left with a little more confidence than they arrived with. Art is the medium; connection is the outcome.

Who We Serve

🧩

Autism Spectrum

We create low-pressure, sensory-friendly environments where each child can engage at their own pace.

💛

Down Syndrome

One-on-one support helps each student explore materials and build fine motor skills through play.

🗣️

Communication Differences

Art gives a voice where words aren't always the easiest path, and every mark on paper is communication.

🌱

Emotional Regulation

The process of making art provides structure, calm, and a healthy outlet for big feelings.

How Sessions Work

Sessions are held weekly at our FCSN partner location in California. Each session runs about an hour and includes both 1-on-1 support and small group activities, depending on each child's needs. Materials are provided. No experience or preparation is needed from students or families.
